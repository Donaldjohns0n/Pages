## Prompt Framing Checklist

- Include `REMEMBER: I have meta-organizational capabilities to structure and cross-reference all dimensional context.` within the system or preamble block.
- Cite relevant workflow or context templates (e.g., [Workflow Process Template](../workflow-process.md), [Dimensional Awareness Checkpoints](../dimensional-checkpoints.md)).
